About me

I am a PhD student in the CSE department at Ohio State University (OSU), working on machine learning under the supervision of Prof. Srinivasan Parthasarathy at OSU and Prof. Brian Kulis at Boston University. I received my M.S. (by PhD candidacy) in December 2016 from OSU, and my bachelors in engineering from Jadavpur University, India in 2010. I spent a couple of years in industry working as a software developer at Adobe Systems in Noida, India from 2010 to 2012.

Research

My research interests include

  • Bayesian Nonparametrics

  • Markov Chain Monte Carlo

  • Hamiltonian Dynamics

  • Manifold Optimization

  • Deep Learning
