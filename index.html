About me

I am a PhD student in the CSE department at Ohio State University (OSU), working on machine learning under the supervision of
Prof. Srinivasan Parthasarathy at OSU and Prof. Brian Kulis at Boston University. I received my M.S. (by PhD candidacy)
in December 2016 from OSU, and my bachelors in engineering from Jadavpur University, India in 2010. I enjoyed awesome internships at
Google (summer 2016) and Adobe Research (summer 2015). Prior to starting my PhD, I spent a couple of years in industry working as a
software developer at Adobe Systems in Noida, India from 2010 to 2012.
